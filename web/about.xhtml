About GOtrack

GOtrack is an integrative tool for exploring changes in gene annotations over time. This is particularly useful when you want to visualize general trends from current annotated genes, as well as information related to a particular gene.

Some interesting features of this tool include:

  1. The number or direct and inferred terms (through propagation to the root of the tree),
  2. The ratio between direct and inferred terms,
  3. A multifunctionality and semantic similarity score,
  4. GO terms associated and the number of associated genes to that term over time and
  5. Changes in the evidence that supports each annotation (publications and evidence codes).

A graph will be displayed in the tab "Count History", displaying information related to your gene product of interest.

Click on the next tab called "Functionality" to select GO terms that have been annotated with the queried gene product

Next, you can explore the section "Global trends" to compare information for two species or compare distinct parameters associated for one species
